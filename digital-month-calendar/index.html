PROGRAMME DETAILS

Weekly Themes

Week 1
May 11 – 17, 2026

Digital Foundations

Kicks off with the Digital Awareness Month launch and a powerful DPD success story showcasing improved project outcomes. Dive into the DX Store. Participate quizzes, riddles and Tech in Action contest to get featured and win exciting prizes.

Digital Month Launch Success Story DX Store
Week 2
May 18 – 24, 2026

Design & Engineering Tech

Dive into Autodesk’s AEC solutions to streamline design management—enabling smoother collaboration, accurate quantity take-offs, and early clash detection that speeds up design cycles and reduces rework. The week continues with a Digital Treasure Hunt, where participants explore a 3D model hands-on and win exciting prizes.

BIM & Digital Twin CAD Automation 3D Simulation Engineering Analytics
Week 3
May 25 – 31, 2026

Construction & Monitoring Tech

Learn how Lean construction tools can deliver smoother execution with stronger planning and project controls. See live demos of 4D simulations, automated DPRs, drone-based progress capture, and dashboards that improve project intelligence and enable faster decision-making.

IoT & Sensors Drone Surveying AI Progress Monitoring EHS Digital
Week 4
June 1 – 5, 2026

Smart Project Delivery, Data insights, AR, VR & Digital Twin

Explore live digital twin and dashboards for real time asset monitoring and predictive maintanance, experience immersive walkthrough through AR/ VR helping early decision and minimise design changes.

AI Project Controls Predictive Analytics Digital Dashboards Data-Driven EPC
